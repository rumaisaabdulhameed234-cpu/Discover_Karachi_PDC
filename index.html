About the Product

Intelligent Health Support, Powered by AI

MediAI is a smart healthcare assistant designed to bridge the gap between patients and medical information. Built on advanced AI infrastructure, it delivers fast, reliable health guidance in multiple languages.

Developed with a focus on accessibility and ease of use, MediAI supports both English and Roman Urdu, making healthcare information available to a broader audience — especially in South Asian communities.

🧠

Describe your symptoms in plain language and receive instant AI-driven analysis with possible conditions ranked by probability.

Important Safety Disclaimer
Please read carefully before using MediAI
  • MediAI is NOT a replacement for a licensed medical doctor or qualified healthcare professional. It does not provide clinical diagnoses.
  • All information provided by MediAI is purely informational and educational in nature and should not be used as the basis for any medical decisions.
  • For any serious, persistent, or emergency health concerns, consult a real doctor or visit a hospital immediately. Do not delay seeking professional care.
  • MediAI's AI suggestions are based on general medical knowledge and may not account for your personal medical history, allergies, or unique health conditions.
  • Medication advice provided is general in nature — always consult a pharmacist or physician before taking any medication, including over-the-counter drugs.
🏥 In case of a medical emergency, please call your local emergency services immediately.
